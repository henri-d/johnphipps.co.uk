CPS Plaque Ceremony

Open Plaque unveiling at Coventry Prep. On Jan 14th 11 am 2022 we will be unveiling two blue plaques at the school. One to commemorate the owner headmasters, Swallow, Sykes, Phipps, the second to Ernest Instone, former chief engineer at Daimler who owned the house before Mr Swallow purchased it in 1921. The unveiling will be carried out by the Lord Mayor of Coventry and there will be speeches from the Jaguar Heritage Trust and former pupils at Sallows/CPS, with a dedication by the KHV111 school chaplain. We will also dedicate a tree planted in memory of John Phipps. The school will be open after the event all former pupils will be most welcome. The Queen and Castle in Kenilworth has been booked for after the event. Please indicate your attendance via the registration form below.
